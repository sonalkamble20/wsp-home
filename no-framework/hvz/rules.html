?? Simulation Overview

+

Humans vs Zombies is a campus-wide evasion simulation. All units deploy as Humans except for one designated "Original Zombie" (OZ). Infectors attempt to assimilate humans. Humans defend with approved foam blasters and socks. Simulation runs 24/7 for one week. Safe Zones: Academic interiors, mess halls during rations, active classrooms, and residence quarters. Outdoor sectors are always active combat zones.

?? Assimilation (Tagging)

+

For Infectors: A tag is a firm touch on any part of the human's body or clothes. Upon contact, demand ID code. Report tag via terminal within 1 hour. Aggression disabled while stunned.

For Humans: Upon contact, you are converted. Transfer bandana to head position. Wait 15 minutes (Transformation Phase) before active hunting.

?? Approved Weaponry

+

Blasters: Nerf-brand or equivalent foam dart launchers. Modifications increasing velocity strictly prohibited. Must pass armory inspection.

Ordnance (Socks): Rolled socks secured with tape/band. Soft throwables only. No weighted cores.

PROHIBITED: Airsoft, paintball, hydro-cannons, melee, riot shields, unauthorized mods.

? Stun Protocol

+

Hit an infector with dart/sock to induce Stun State. Stunned units must kneel/sit and initiate 15-minute reboot timer. Movement/Tagging disabled. Honesty is mandatory. Disputes logged with Game Control. Sequential hits do no stack duration.

Hazardous (Open Play):

?? Patient Zero (OZ)

+

OZ begins infection. ID: CLASSIFIED for initial 48h. Wears bandana on arm (Stealth Mode). After 48h or 3 confirmed tags, masking fails - must wear on head. OZ immune to starvation for 72h.

?? Starvation Sequence

+

Infectors must feed (tag) once every 48h or face elimination. Starved units reboot as "Spectators" (Mission participation allowed, Hunting disabled). Prevents camping/hiding strategies.
